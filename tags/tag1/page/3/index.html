Appendix D Equipment: Additional Details

A. Details Regarding Illegal Equipment 1. Hard or unyielding substances are permitted, if covered, only to protect an injury. 2. Hand and arm protectors (covered casts or splints) are permitted only to protect a fracture or dislocation. 3. Thigh guards may not be made of any hard substances, unless all surfaces are covered with material such as closed-cell vinyl foam that is at least 1⁄4-inch thick on the outside surface and at least 3⁄8-inch thick on the inside surface and the overlaps of the edges. »
